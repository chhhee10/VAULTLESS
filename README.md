## 🔐 The Problem: The Secret Crisis

Digital security still depends too heavily on secrets that can be **stolen, guessed, phished, leaked, or coerced** out of people. Passwords, OTPs, and even many forms of 2FA fail at the exact moment a person is under pressure.

VAULTLESS explores a safer model: **authentication based on how a person naturally behaves**, not just what they know. Instead of trusting a static secret, the system looks at behavioural signals — typing rhythm, mouse movement, touch behaviour, and device motion — then uses that signal to make authentication harder to fake and easier to adapt under real-world conditions.

---

## 🌍 Social Impact: Human-Centric Security

VAULTLESS is designed as a prototype for security that protects people, not just accounts. By shifting from "what you know" to "who you are," we mitigate the most common attack vectors while providing a safety net for users in high-stakes environments.

| Target Demographic | Value Proposition |
|---|---|
| **Digital Nomads** | Eliminates risk of physical shoulder surfing for passwords. |
| **Crypto Asset Holders** | Adds a behavioural signature layer to wallet interactions. |
| **Activists & Journalists** | Coercion-aware duress flows protect data without alerting the aggressor. |
| **Elderly Users** | Simplifies access by removing the cognitive load of complex passwords. |

The **duress flow** is a core philosophical pillar: in a coercive situation, the goal is not only to block access but to protect the user without obviously alerting the attacker — making VAULTLESS a prototype for more humane security.

## 🧠 The Core Philosophy: Zero-Secret Identity

VAULTLESS operates on a **Zero-Secret** principle. We don't store your password because there is no password. We don't store your biometrics because they are converted into a mathematical "helper" used only to reconstruct keys at the moment of authentication. 

Your identity is not a static string in a database; it is a live, probabilistic signal generated by your interaction with the device.

---

## ✅ Behavioural DNA: The Multi-Signal Engine

VAULTLESS replaces static credentials with a dynamic **Behavioural DNA** profile built from high-frequency sensor data. This multi-layered approach ensures that even if one signal is spoofed, the others will fail to match.

| State | Score Threshold | System Response |
|---|---|---|
| ✅ **Authenticated** | `> 0.70` | Access granted to the main dashboard. |
| 🟡 **Duress** | `0.60 – 0.70` (+ Stress) | Ghost session loaded; Silent alerts dispatched. |
| ❌ **Rejected** | `< 0.60` | Access denied; Failed attempt logged on-chain. |

## 🎹 Keystroke Dynamics: The Rhythm of Thought

Keystroke dynamics is the study of the unique "cadence" of a person's typing. VAULTLESS captures precise timing events with sub-millisecond precision using `performance.now()`.

### Metrics Captured:
1.  **Dwell Time (Hold Time)**: The duration between a `KeyDown` and `KeyUp` event. This measures the physical "weight" of a person's typing.
2.  **Flight Time (Latency)**: The duration between a `KeyUp` of one key and the `KeyDown` of the next. This measures the cognitive transition speed between specific character pairings (digraphs).
3.  **Rhythm Variance**: The consistency of the typing speed across the entire phrase, used to detect abnormal stress or automation.

```text
+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------+
|   S   |   e   |   c   |   u   |   r   |   e   |
+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------+

Temporal Events:
  Key Down (S) -----> Key Up (S)      Key Down (e) -----> Key Up (e)
      |                  |                |                  |
      +-------HOLD-------+                +-------HOLD-------+
                         |                |
                         +-----FLIGHT-----+
```

## 🖱️ Mouse & Gesture Dynamics: Spatial Intelligence

On desktop, mouse movement is a powerful second-factor biometric. On mobile, this transitions to touch gestures and device orientation.

### Spatial Metrics:
- **Velocity & Acceleration**: How fast do you move the pointer between elements?
- **Angle Variance**: The "smoothness" or "jaggedness" of your motion paths.
- **Touch Pressure & Area**: (Mobile only) How much surface area of your finger touches the screen during a swipe.
- **Inertial Signatures**: (Mobile only) The micro-jitters recorded by the Gyroscope and Accelerometer while you interact with the UI.

These signals are fused with keystroke data to create a 64-dimension feature vector.

## 🧬 Fuzzy Extractor: Stable Keys from Noisy DNA

Biometrics are inherently "noisy"—you never type or move exactly the same way twice. Standard hashing (like SHA-256) would fail immediately because a single millisecond difference changes the entire hash.

VAULTLESS uses a **Fuzzy Extractor** architecture (based on Error Correction Codes) to solve this:

1.  **Enrollment (Gen)**: We take your DNA vector and generate two things: a **Secret Key** and a **Helper Data** (Public) string.
2.  **Authentication (Rep)**: When you authenticate later with a *slightly different* DNA vector, we use the **Helper Data** to "correct" the noise in your new sample.
3.  **Reconstruction**: If your new sample is within the "Fuzzy" threshold, the extractor reproduces the exact same **Secret Key** as enrollment.

This allows us to generate a deterministic cryptographic key from a non-deterministic human movement.

## 📐 Z-Score Normalisation: Mathematical Identity

To handle users typing at different speeds, we apply **Z-Score Normalisation**. This strips away the absolute speed (the "mean") and focuses entirely on the relative rhythm (the "shape").

```text
Formula: z = (value - mean) / standard_deviation
```

This ensures that whether you are typing fast or slow, your **relative rhythm** remains constant and recognizable by the engine.

## 📋 The Enrollment Protocol: Building the Baseline

Enrollment is a rigorous 3-step process to establish your high-confidence baseline.

1.  **Sample Acquisition**: User types the phrase 3 times.
2.  **Centroid Calculation**: We compute the mathematical center (mean) of all samples to eliminate outliers.
3.  **Helper Generation**: The Fuzzy Extractor generates the public helper string.
4.  **On-Chain Registry**: The helper string is stored on the Solana blockchain in your Identity PDA.

---

## 🧠 The Scoring Engine: 4-Component Weighted Analysis

Our proprietary scoring engine evaluates authentication attempts across four distinct dimensions:

| Component | Weight | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Hold Shape** | 40% | Relative finger weight pattern. |
| **Hold Magnitude** | 25% | Absolute duration proportions. |
| **Flight Shape** | 25% | Cognitive transition rhythm. |
| **Overall Pace** | 10% | Global speed consistency. |

If a match is found, the system proceeds to reconstruct your session key.

## 🔄 End-to-End Workflow: The Lifecycle of a Session

```text
  [ START ]
      |
  Enrollment (3 samples) -> Generate Helper Data -> Store on Solana PDA
      |
  Authentication (1 sample) -> Use Helper Data to "Correct" Noise
      |
      +---> [MATCH] -> Reconstruct Key -> Access Dashboard
      |
      +---> [DURESS] -> Log Event -> Dispatch EmailJS -> Load Ghost Session
      |
      +---> [FAIL] -> Log Rejection -> Deny Access
```

## ⛓️ Blockchain Layer: PDA-Based Identity Registry

VAULTLESS utilizes **Program Derived Addresses (PDAs)** on Solana to manage identity states.

- **Identity PDA**: Deterministically derived from `["identity", user_pubkey]`.
- **Immutable Log**: Every success, failure, and duress event is recorded on-chain.
- **Decentralized Storage**: No central database holds your "helper data"—it lives in the global Solana ledger.

---

## 👻 The Duress Protocol: Silent Protection & Ghost Sessions

If a user is under coercion, VAULTLESS activates a **Silent Duress Protocol**.

- **Solana Flag**: A permanent `DuressEvent` is logged on-chain.
- **Ghost Session**: The user is granted access to a "Ghost Dashboard" with fake balances and activity. The attacker is satisfied, but the real assets remain untouchable.

---

## 📧 Automated Response: EmailJS Security Notifications

Immediate real-time alerts are sent via **EmailJS** when duress is triggered.

- **Aesthetic**: Premium, minimalist "Security Protocol" design.
- **Payload**: Contains full transaction hashes, wallet addresses, and recovery links.

## 📜 Smart Contract Reference: Anchor Logic

The contract (Anchor/Rust) manages the global registry:
- `initialize_identity`: Bootstraps the user's biometric state.
- `update_identity`: Refines the biometric profile.
- `authenticate`: Logs a successful behavioural match.
- `trigger_duress`: Records security breaches for the recovery network.
