Cryptography

The Gesture DNA system applies Fuzzy Extractor theory (Dodis, Reyzin, Smith — EUROCRYPT 2004) to keystroke biometrics. Your behavioral pattern is error-corrected by BCH codes and converted to a reproducible key by HKDF-SHA256.

  • Pearson correlation on hold/flight Z-scores — 65% scoring weight
  • BCH error-correction tolerates natural variation across sessions
  • Stress detection: variance > 2.5× enrolled triggers duress mode
  • AES-256-GCM encrypts the session secret at rest in Chrome storage
  • Burned nullifiers prevent replay attacks on Solana devnet
